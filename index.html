Who We Are

A trusted partner
in healthcare supply chains

SINA Enterprise is a healthcare-focused procurement company built on a simple principle: hospitals and medical institutions should never struggle to secure the supplies they need to care for patients.

We participate in government tenders and competitive bidding processes on behalf of, and independently for, healthcare institutions. Once awarded, we manage the full procurement cycle: sourcing, logistics, delivery, and documentation.

Our singular focus on the healthcare industry means we understand the regulatory requirements, urgency, and quality standards that medical procurement demands.

What We Do

End-to-end procurement, handled

From identifying the tender opportunity to delivering the final shipment, SINA Enterprise manages every step of the healthcare procurement process.

01

Tender Identification & Bidding

We monitor and identify relevant government and institutional tenders in the healthcare space, preparing competitive, compliant bids with precision.

02

Medical Supply Procurement

Medications, surgical consumables, diagnostics, and general medical supplies sourced from verified, quality-assured suppliers at competitive prices.

03

Contract Fulfilment & Delivery

Once a contract is awarded, we manage sourcing, logistics, and on-time delivery, ensuring every commitment to the institution is met.

04

Supplier Relationship Management

We maintain a vetted network of healthcare product manufacturers and distributors, enabling rapid sourcing even for specialised items.

05

Compliance & Documentation

Full documentation support, from regulatory filings to delivery receipts, ensuring institutions meet their audit and compliance requirements.

06

Consultation & Advisory

Not sure where to begin? We advise healthcare institutions on procurement planning, budgeting, and supplier evaluation.
